About Orcus

Hi there! We’re Abin (Alex) and Harshith two passionate 18-year-olds who just finished high school and we’re incredibly excited to introduce you to Orcus.

We created Orcus because we truly believe that India’s young generation has the power to build amazing things-they just need the right guidance, resources, and inspiration. Our dream is to spark a vibrant startup culture across the country, starting with young dreamers like us.

At Orcus, we’re on a mission to make all kinds of startup knowledge accessible, relatable, and exciting. Whether it’s about turning ideas into real businesses, building strong teams, raising funds, or overcoming challenges, we want to share everything we know-and everything we’re still learning on our journey too.

We’re here to empower young minds to think big, take bold steps, and create the future they imagine. This is just the beginning for us, and we’re thrilled to have you with us as we grow, learn, and build together.

Let’s make something incredible happen.

-Abin (Alex) & Harshith,
 Founders of Orcus
