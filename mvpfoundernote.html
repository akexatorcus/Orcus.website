MVP Guidelines for startups!

Orcus.wiki

◉ Fast

Prioritize speed in performance and delivery.

◉ Not Fancy

Focus on functionality over aesthetics.
Focus on delivering a clean, simple, and functional experience.

◉ Iterate

Build quickly, gather feedback, and continuously improve.

◉ Opinion of User

Make user feedback the cornerstone of your development process.
Engage with users regularly to understand their needs, pain points, and preferences.
Ensure the product evolves to align closely with what users truly want.
