Unicorpse

Orcus.wiki

A Unicorpse in a startup refers to a company that was once considered a unicorn, meaning it had reached a valuation of over $1 billion, but has since failed, gone bankrupt, or significantly declined in value. The term is used to describe the remnants of a once-promising startup that has not lived up to its initial expectations, often serving as a cautionary tale for entrepreneurs and investors.
