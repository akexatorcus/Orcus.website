IPR Policy for Orcus

This Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) Policy outlines Orcus’s approach to content, trademarks, designs, and inventions, reflecting our intent to comply with Indian statutory requirements as we grow. Currently, Orcus has not completed formal registrations for any intellectual property, but we are committed to protecting our assets and guiding our community on IPR best practices.

1. Ownership of Content

All textual content, graphics, logos, and other materials published on Orcus (the “Website”) are owned by Orcus. Under the Copyright Act, 1957, copyright protection exists automatically upon creation, even without formal registration. Unauthorized reproduction or distribution remains prohibited.

2. Unregistered Trademarks

The name “Orcus” and the Orcus logo are Orcus’s trademarks, although they are not yet registered under the Trademarks Act, 1999. We intend to pursue formal registration as we scale. Until then, usage without Orcus’s prior written approval is not permitted.

3. Potential Patentable Inventions

Orcus may develop inventions, processes, or improvements that could qualify for protection under the Patents Act, 1970. We are in the process of evaluating our patent strategy. Any invention conceived by Orcus should be considered proprietary, and disclosure requires prior consent.

4. Design Intentions

Original product designs and graphical user interface (GUI) layouts created by Orcus are intended for registration under the Designs Act, 2000. While pending, these designs are treated as Orcus intellectual property and may not be copied or used without authorization.

5. Geographical Indications Collaboration

Orcus may work with local products bearing Geographical Indications (GIs) under the Geographical Indications Act, 1999. Such GI products remain protected by their respective registrations, and any collaboration agreements will respect GI terms.

6. Reporting Infringement

If you believe that your IPR has been infringed on this Website, please send a written notice with details of the infringement to orcuswiki@gmail.com. Orcus will investigate and take appropriate action consistent with Indian IPR law.

Effective Date: April 28, 2025

By using the Website, you acknowledge this Policy and agree to respect Orcus’s intellectual property, both registered and unregistered, in accordance with applicable Indian laws.
